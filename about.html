Discover EU program

DiscoverEU is erasmus programm that allows young adults travel around Europe. This teaches young adults how to manage your finances, explor diffrent cities,maintain good teamwork and learn new languages.

Historical Landmarks & Architecture

Explore Lithuania’s medieval castles (like Trakai), Germany’s grand palaces (Neuschwanstein), Poland’s historic Old Towns (Kraków), Austria’s imperial Schönbrunn Palace, the Czech Republic’s Prague Castle, France’s Eiffel Tower, and Latvia’s Riga Art Nouveau district.

Diverse Cultural Experience

Each country offers unique traditions, from Lithuania’s folklore festivals to Germany’s Oktoberfest, Poland’s pierogi-making culture, Austria’s classical music heritage, Czech Republic’s beer culture, France’s haute cuisine, and Latvia’s midsummer celebrations

Efficient travel Experience

Most of the countries are connceted with high-speed trains, busses and cheap airline tickets

Affordable & Luxury Travel Options

From budget-friendly hostels in Poland and Latvia to luxury stays in Paris or Vienna, travelers can experience both cost-effective and high-end options across these destinations.

Rich WWII & Cold War History

Visit historically significant sites like Lithuania’s KGB Museum, Germany’s Berlin Wall, Poland’s Auschwitz, Austria’s Mauthausen, the Czech Republic’s Terezín, France’s D-Day beaches, and Latvia’s Occupation Museum.

Culinary Delights

Savor Lithuania’s cepelinai, Germany’s bratwurst, Poland’s bigos, Austria’s schnitzel, Czech Republic’s svíčková, France’s croissants and cheese, and Latvia’s grey peas with bacon.

Festivals & Events

Experience Lithuania’s Užgavėnės (pre-Lent festival), Germany’s Christmas markets, Poland’s St. Dominic’s Fair, Austria’s Vienna Opera Ball, the Czech Republic’s Karlovy Vary Film Festival, France’s Cannes Film Festival, and Latvia’s Song and Dance Festival.
