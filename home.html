Hello explorer!

My name is Karīna and i have traveled since I was four-years-old. I have been to three Erasmus programs and traveled with my family. In this website i would like to share my experience with you and share advice that I think would be interesting and useful to know. I hope this website will inspire you to plan a vacation elsewhere anytime soon.

Important!

Check your passport or ID card when you leave the house. Also check the expiering date of your documents. Check twice the information before buying the tickets. Don`t forget your phone charger

Suitcase

Check the weather news in your destination before leaving and pack clothes that can be used in more than one outfit. Accesories for me is a must, so I advice you, take some funky sunglasses, it will give you core memories :) I like to take a book with me and read sometimes.

Search for cheap tickets

Searching for cheaper tickets can be difficult depending of the destination, but better cheaper tickets and R&B, so you have more money to spend in exploring culture and food.
